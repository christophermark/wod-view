WOD VIEW

Support

WOD View turns the workout-history export from SugarWOD into a fast, private training archive that lives entirely on your phone. No account, no servers — see the privacy policy.

Getting your workout history into the app

  1. In the SugarWOD app or website, open your profile and request your data export ("Request My Data"). SugarWOD emails it to you — usually within a few minutes.
  2. Open that email on your iPhone and save the workouts.csv attachment to Files.
  3. In WOD View, tap Import (on the welcome screen, or later in Settings) and pick that file.

Want to look around first? Preview mode on the welcome screen loads three years of sample data. Your real import is one tap away whenever you're ready.

Common issues

  • "That file isn't a SugarWOD export." — Make sure you picked the workouts.csv attachment from SugarWOD's email, not a different CSV or a screenshot of it.
  • New workouts aren't in the app. — The app shows a snapshot of your last export. Request a fresh export from SugarWOD and re-import it from Settings; the new file replaces the old one.
  • Starting over. — Settings → Clear My Data removes everything from the device. Your SugarWOD account is never touched.
